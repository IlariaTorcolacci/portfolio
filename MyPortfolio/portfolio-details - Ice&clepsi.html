ICE&CLEPSI

Air purifiers that blend funtionality with elegance
OVERVIEW

During my Bachelor's degree at @ISIA Firence, I had the opportunity to collaborate with @Ariete on developing a new line of home sanitizing products.

Responding to the significant shift in user needs and the global scenario post-pandemic, our team designed an innovative product for air purification, intended to improve the quality of living for students and workers.

Project report
Goal
Design a new physical device and the the mobile app for a new target
My Role
Product and service concept development, 3D modeling and rendering, high-fidelity physical prototyping
Design focus
User research, competitor analysis, interaction design, UX, UI, Industrial design
Timeline
4 months, 3 people
OUR APPROACH

  • Define the project's objective by identifying a new range of sanitizing products with a mobile interface
  • Conducted research on existing air purifier products, user needs, and current market trends and future scenarios
  • Development of various concepts for the air purifier, likely involving sketches, preliminary designs, and functional ideas
  • Design the form, aesthetics, physical components of the air purifier and the user interface for the mobile app
  • Product prototyping involved developing the design through 3D modeling, 3D printing the prototypes, and then testing them to identify issues

Design of the product

After the pandemic, it became important to live in an increasingly sanitized environment. We focused on the development of two single-use purifiers, dedicated to two types of targets: students and workers. The purifiers are equipped with activated carbon and HEPA filter, more durable. They are equipped with an application that monitors air quality (AQI) providing a database of the purifier activities and a display integrated into the product for direct control.

UX EXPERIENCE OF THE PRODUCT

We focused more on the user experience of the product. Making the communication of the object, through its controls as easy and intuitive as possible for the user. The air purifier has a display on which you can interact with the machine, adjusting the fan power and activating the night mode. If it is not possible to physically interact with the object, it is possible, through the application, to activate the air purifier and access all its functions remotely. The application contains the activity of the purifier in a database and monitors the level of AQI present in the environment. It also informs the user when it is time to change the filter.
