ITA Airways

Ergonomics Applied to the Design of Usable Web Pages and Apps
OVERVIEW

The redesign aims to transform the perception of the @ITAAirways website from a crowded and cluttered web page into an intuitive, user-centered platform.

The aim is to provide users with a smooth, pleasant, and easily accessible experience aligned with user needs and expectations.

Goal
Redesign the the main flows of the ITA Airways Website
My Role
Conducting UX research, testing and data analysis, I took care of the final UI, developing the design system, and prototyping the main user flows.
Design focus
Usability testing, informational architecture, UX, UI, Competitive analysis
Timeline
4 months
OUR APPROACH

  • Identified UX pain points through competitor and heuristic analysis
  • Streamlined flight selection with improved filtering and clearer hierarchy
  • Redesigned benefits section for better visibility and decision-making
  • Aligned offers page behavior with user expectations for intuitive use
  • Validated redesign through user testing and SEQ evaluation
  • Delivered a responsive prototype focused on clarity, accessibility, and ease of use

TASK MATRIX

Starting from a detailed analysis of user needs, we defined their priorities and identified three main tasks. Subsequently, we conducted a heuristic evaluation to measure the clicks required to complete each individual operation, with the goal of improving navigation fluidity

REDESIGN GOAL

After analyzing the findings both from the Heuristics, user tasks and competitor analysis, we prioritized 3 improvements to our redesign. Making simpler structure of the website and more accessible and user-friendly.
